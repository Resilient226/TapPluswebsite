How It Works

Three steps.
Seamlessly.

No app download. No friction for your customer. No training required for your staff.

I
📲

The Tap

Your staff carry an NFC card. A customer holds their phone near it. Their browser opens in an instant — no app, no login, no friction whatsoever.

II
⭐

The Rating

A fully branded page appears. They select their rating. Five stars redirects automatically to Google or Yelp. Anything lower is captured privately for your team only.

III
📊

The Intelligence

Your Google rating climbs. Managers see real-time performance dashboards. AI coaching reaches each staff member directly. The entire operation improves.

way you work.

01 — AI

Coaching Intelligence

Every staff member receives personalized AI analysis — specific strengths, clear areas for growth, and a direct coaching message grounded in their own performance data.

Team Leaderboard

Real-time performance rankings across your entire team. Know exactly who is driving reviews, who needs support, and how the team is performing this week.

Rating Estimator

Input your current Google rating and your target. We calculate precisely how many five-star reviews you need, and how long it will take your team to get there.
